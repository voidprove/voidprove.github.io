Local Info

All conference events are placed at the Peking University Shaoyuan (勺园) Guest House on campus.

The public lecture by Professor Gao will be in the Zhihua Building hosting the mathematics department.

Due to the high demand for hotels around campus, we cannot make block reservations for regular participants. All regular participants are encouraged to book their own room as soon as possible. 由于北京大学周边酒店客房紧张，我们无法为一般参会人员统一预留酒店房间，恳请有意参会的同学老师尽快自行预定房间！

Nearby hotels include:

  1. Zhongguan xinyuan Global Village （中关新园）
  2. Beijing Posts and Telecommunications Nursing Home （北京邮电会议中心）
